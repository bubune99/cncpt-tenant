## MVP FEATURES (Phase 1)

### Core Product Information
- Product Name/Title with SEO optimization
- Product Description with rich text editor
- Short Description/Excerpt for listings
- Product SKU (unique identifier)
- Product Categories (hierarchical)
- Product Tags for filtering
- Product Brand/Manufacturer

### Basic Media Management
- Multiple product images with gallery
- Featured image selection
- Image alt text for SEO/accessibility
- Basic image zoom functionality

### Essential Pricing & Inventory
- Regular price and sale price
- Basic stock management (in stock/out of stock)
- Stock quantity tracking
- Low stock alerts

### Product Variations (Basic)
- Simple product variations (size, color)
- Basic product attributes
- Variable pricing by variation

### Basic SEO Features
- SEO title and meta description
- Custom URL slugs
- Basic Open Graph tags

### Fundamental Shipping
- Product weight and dimensions
- Basic shipping class assignment
- Simple shipping cost calculation

### Customer Experience Essentials
- Product search functionality
- Basic filtering (category, price range)
- Simple sorting (price, name, date)
- Basic product reviews and ratings

### Administrative Basics
- Product status management (draft, published)
- Basic bulk actions (edit, delete)
- Simple CSV import/export
